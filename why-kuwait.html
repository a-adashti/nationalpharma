Key Market Drivers

  • High Per Capita Spending: Kuwait maintains an exceptional per capita healthcare investment index, ensuring a constant demand for innovative premium treatments and device portfolios.
  • Demographic Shifts: A steady rise in population density, combined with a growing aging demographic, accelerates public demands for modern therapeutic options.
  • Infrastructure Modernization: Over 20 mega hospital extension and construction projects currently backed by the Ministry of Health to expand operational bed capacities.
