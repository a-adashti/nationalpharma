Corporate Governance & Founders

The leadership driving National Pharma's commitment to strategic healthcare growth and strict regulatory excellence in the State of Kuwait.

Ali Dashti

Co-Founder & Executive Director

Ali H. Dashti is an entrepreneur and investor with extensive experience in founding, developing, and leading businesses across various industries. He is committed to driving sustainable growth through business development, corporate expansion, and the establishment of strategic partnerships.

Ali has a strong ability to identify promising investment opportunities and transform them into successful, sustainable ventures, with a focus on strengthening corporate governance, fostering innovation, and creating long-term value. Through his entrepreneurial vision and leadership, he continues to contribute to strengthening National Pharma's position in the healthcare sector while supporting its mission to provide high-quality pharmaceutical products, medical devices, healthcare solutions, and exceptional services that meet the needs of customers and partners.

Founder's Vision

"At National Pharma, our expansion is driven by a clear entrepreneurial vision: to transform high-potential healthcare opportunities into sustainable, value-creating realities. We believe that true corporate leadership is achieved by fostering innovation and establishing strong, strategic alliances. Our commitment is to continually strengthen our corporate governance and market position, ensuring that National Pharma remains the partner of choice for those seeking to deliver exceptional, high-quality healthcare solutions to the State of Kuwait."

Ibrahim Hussain

Co-Founder & General Manager

Ibrahim is the Co-Founder and General Manager of the company, bringing over 15 years of experience in the pharmaceutical, medical equipment, and medical supplies industry.

He has extensive expertise in business development, commercial operations, and working closely with the Ministry of Health, as well as public and private hospitals. He has built strong professional relationships across the healthcare sector and possesses in-depth knowledge of regulatory requirements, procurement processes, and the medical market. His experience and industry network play a key role in supporting the company’s growth and strengthening its position in the healthcare sector.

Manager's Message

"Sustainable growth in the pharmaceutical and medical device sector requires more than market presence—it demands deep-rooted industry relationships and an absolute alignment with regulatory standards. Having spent over 15 years working closely with the Ministry of Health and the wider medical market, my focus is to ensure our operations transition seamlessly from high-level procurement to effective public and private sector delivery. We bridge the gap between global healthcare supply and Kuwait's local demand with reliability and expertise."
